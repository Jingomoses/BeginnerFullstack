"The Battle for supremacy"

There is an ongoing battle for supremacy in the air. A battle of technologies. This battle is between the two world's biggest avaiation powerhouses.

  • Boeing
  • Airbus

While both companies boast of having the latest aviation technology, each powerhouse has got something that gives it an edge over the other.

Boeing

Boeing fanatics usually say - "if it's not Boeing, I'm not going". Well, they believe in the machines made at Boeing. Most famous at Boeing, is the "Queen of the Skies" - the Boeing 747, with all her variants, from the 200, the more famous 400, and now the latest "Dash-8", the 747-800


Another bird that has of recent won the hearts of the Boeing fans is the "787 - Dreamliner" which is set to become the latest longhaul workhorse in the Boeing family, and the "737 - Max"another short to medium range marvel, all products of American technology.

Airbus

Airbus is a combination of European technologies. From "Setting the standards" as one of their slogans, Airbus has since "made it fly" the Airbus A380, the world's wholly double decker giant, the world's biggest commercial plane


The A380 having taken over as the giant of the skies boasts of two seating levels along the entire fuselage, record breaking legroom and space not seen before on any commercial plane. This has turned out to be the favorite for those that love their space. Airbus has also recently rolled out the A350 many see as one to replace the A330 and A340 variants, and again as a competitor on medium and longhaul flights all packed with state of the art technology from Airbus.

Comparison & Verdict

In business terms, the best company would be one that is making the biggest profits off of it's production line even though customers have a preference with regard to comfort, the make, product designs. So, customers could vote otherwise too with respect to their experience at 40,000ft
